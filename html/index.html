Sampler tip #1: Set 'max files'-value to the range of your 'start-sample-offset'-knob on your sampler, it will allow you to easily switch samples.

Sampler tip #2: Pitching up audio is a trick to save samplespace on hardware samplers (just turn down the pitch on the sampler during playback). Please note that this introduces a (sometimes desirable) bitcrush sound on material with high frequencies.

Sampler tip #3:If your samplestart-knobrange varies from 0..127, set 'Max files' to 32 files for a directory of similar-length one-bar beatloops, to eventually have 4 bar-slices of each sample on your sampler (128/4).
