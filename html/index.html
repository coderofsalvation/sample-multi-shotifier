Sampler tip #1:Set 'max files'-value to the range of your 'start-sample-offset'-knob on your sampler, it will allow you to easily switch samples.

Sampler tip #2:Pitching up audio is a trick to save samplespace on the electribe (just turn down the pitch on the electribe for playback). Please note that this introduces a bitcrush sound on material with high frequencies.
